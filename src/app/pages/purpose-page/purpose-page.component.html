Necessity is the mother of invention
The purpose of ODIN is to offer a freely-available alternative method of social communication on the Internet that is superior to the current arrangement of predominantly for-profit communication and social networking services.

ODIN is a free and open-source software project for the creation and upkeep of a decentralized computer network which will ultimately serve as a globally available human identity authentication service, among other things.

By virtue of the existence of this highly-available utilitarian network, every person in the world is offered the opportunity to participate in an uncensorable global community in which each person possesses full ownership and privacy of their digital identity.
